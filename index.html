About Me

I am Akshay Anand, a Research Scholar in Computer Science and Engineering Department at the National Institute of Technology Calicut, specializing in Responsible and Explainable AI. My research focuses on developing AI models that are not only accurate but also transparent, ethical, and interpretable.

Previously, I served as an Assistant Professor (on contract) at KBM Government College Thalassery, where I mentored students in emerging AI technologies. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science (AI) from Kannur University with distinction and am currently pursuing my PhD in AI at NIT Calicut.

Recognized with NTA UGC NET JRF and Lectureship certifications, I actively contribute to the advancement of AI through research publications, academic collaborations, and innovative solutions bridging theory and practice.
